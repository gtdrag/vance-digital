Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2025

vance.digital ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our websites and products.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — name, email address, and other details you provide when creating an account or contacting us.
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely through Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data — information about how you interact with our products, including pages visited, features used, and device information.
  • Cookies — small data files stored on your device to improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our products and services
  • Process transactions and send billing confirmations
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you
  • Improve our products and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Vercel — website hosting and deployment.
  • Yahoo — fantasy sports data integration (for Roto Agent).

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit and secure storage practices. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@vance.digital.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
